About Author

VIMMI OM MEHTA is a writer and management professional. She has a bachelor’s degree in electrical engineering and an executive MBA from IIM Calcutta in leadership management. Over the past twenty-eight years, she has worked in various fields including engineering, procurement, finance, audit and HR.
